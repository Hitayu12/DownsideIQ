Platform Overview

Live short-horizon (12–24h) equity downside-risk engine — prediction is not the edge, risk-controlled decision-making is.

How it works

A Data Intelligence Council gathers price, volatility, market context, timestamp-safe fundamentals and live news. A Prediction & Risk Council runs an XGBoost downside classifier, a GARCH volatility model, and a quantile tail model. A capped log-odds news overlay adjusts the base probability, then a mode-aware risk engine applies gates, limits and a kill switch to emit SHORT WATCH or NO TRADE — every signal logged, explained and reconciled.
